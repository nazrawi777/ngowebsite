Hulegeb

Weaving Dignity, Crafting Independence

For over 70 years, Hulegeb has been led by and for Ethiopia’s blind and physically disabled community — turning skill into steady, dignified livelihoods.

We preserve 28,277 m2 of endowed land for social purpose

more than an organization

A disability-led social enterprise

Hulegeb provides vocational training, dignified employment, and rights-based advocacy for blind and physically disabled Ethiopians.

how it works

From training to sustainable work

Our step-by-step model: identify skills, train, produce market-ready goods, and reinvest proceeds into training and advocacy.

Train

Immersive, hands-on training that prepares participants for paid work.

Produce

Workshops function as production businesses where artisans make market-ready goods.

Operate

Run milling and production services that serve the community and create revenue.

Advocate & Report

Use evidence and experience to promote disability rights and inclusive policy.
