What We Offer

Our comprehensive services create pathways to independence through skills training, production, community services, and systemic advocacy.

Service 1

Vocational Training & Skills Development

We provide hands-on, adaptive training programs that prepare individuals for sustainable livelihoods.

  • Weaving & Carpet Making: Traditional hand-weaving techniques using natural wool and cotton
  • Woodworking & Furniture Crafting: From basic carpentry to custom furniture production
  • Artisan Craft Production: Brush, broom, mop, and household item manufacturing
  • Textile Production: Bedding, curtains, towels, and fabric weaving
  • Milling Operations: Grain and material processing using modern equipment
  • Learn-by-Doing Model: Trainees work directly in production workshops
  • Adapted Tools & Workstations: Customized for varying abilities
  • Mentor-Led Instruction: Experienced artisans guide each learner
  • Entrepreneurship Training: Business skills for self-employment
  • Workplace Safety & Quality Control: Professional standards education

Outcome: Graduates are equipped for employment within Hulegeb, with other employers, or to start their own enterprises.

Service 2

Milling Service

Our community-focused milling operation supports local food security while providing employment and training.

  • 5 Modern Milling Machines: Efficient processing of grains and materials
  • Operated by Trained Artisans: Including blind and physically disabled individuals
  • Affordable & Accessible: Serving local households and small businesses
  • Dual Purpose Platform: Functional service + vocational training environment
  • Provides essential community service
  • Generates operational revenue
  • Offers practical vocational experience
  • Demonstrates inclusive employment in action
Service 3

Advocacy & Awareness Services

We work to transform systems, perceptions, and policies to create a more inclusive society.

  • Policy Engagement: Collaborating with government bodies on disability-inclusive legislation
  • Community Awareness: Workshops and campaigns to challenge stereotypes
  • Corporate Inclusion Consulting: Advising businesses on accessible hiring and workplaces
  • Media Partnership: Amplifying disabled voices and stories
  • Network Building: Connecting disability organizations for collective impact
  • Increased public awareness of disability rights
  • More inclusive local and national policies
  • Greater private sector engagement with disability inclusion
  • Stronger disability community representation
Service 4

Production & Manufacturing Services

Our workshops produce high-quality, market-ready goods while functioning as living classrooms.

  • Carpet & Rug Weaving: Custom and standard sizes
  • Furniture Manufacturing: Custom orders and standard pieces
  • Household Goods: Brushes, mops, brooms, and textiles
  • Contract Manufacturing: Custom production for businesses and organizations
  • For Businesses: Ethically produced, high-quality goods with social impact
  • For Trainees: Real-world production experience
  • For Community: Sustainable employment opportunities
Service 5

Custom Design & Manufacturing

We collaborate with clients to create bespoke products that meet specific needs.

  • Product Development: From concept to finished product
  • Material Selection: Sustainable, local material sourcing
  • Adaptive Design: Products designed for accessibility
  • Bulk Order Production: For retailers, hotels, offices, and institutions
Service 6

Partnership & Capacity Building Services

We share our model and expertise with organizations seeking to enhance their disability inclusion.

  • Organizational Consultations: On disability-inclusive programming
  • Training of Trainers: Building capacity in other institutions
  • Model Replication Support: Adapting Hulegeb's approach for new contexts
  • Impact Assessment: Disability inclusion evaluation and planning
